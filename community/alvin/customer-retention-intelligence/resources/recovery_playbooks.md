# Recovery Playbooks

> Step-by-step recovery strategies for common high-churn scenarios. Check `churn_indicators.md` for risk score before selecting a playbook.

---

## Playbook 1: Payment / Billing Failure

**Trigger:** Failed payment, unexpected charge, overcharge, or post-cancellation billing.

1. Open with empathy — do NOT open with troubleshooting
2. Verify the charge in the billing system within the same session
3. Explain what happened clearly, without jargon
4. Offer immediate resolution: refund or credit (whichever is faster)
5. Communicate the refund timeline explicitly (e.g., "2–3 business days")
6. Add goodwill credit if frustration score ≥ 7
7. Follow up 48 hours after refund confirmation

| Severity | Compensation |
|---|---|
| Overcharge < $20 | Full refund + $10 credit |
| Overcharge $20–$100 | Full refund + 1 month free |
| Overcharge > $100 | Full refund + 2–3 months free + manager call |

---

## Playbook 2: Repeat Contact (Same Issue)

**Trigger:** Customer contacts 3+ times about the same unresolved issue.

1. Own the failure: *"I owe you an apology — this should have been resolved sooner."*
2. Do NOT ask them to re-explain — review ticket history first
3. Assign one dedicated agent to this issue through resolution
4. Commit to a specific resolution date/time and stick to it
5. Proactively update the customer every 24 hours — don't wait for them to follow up
6. Offer compensation post-resolution

| Contacts | Compensation |
|---|---|
| 3 contacts | Apology + $10–$25 credit |
| 4–5 contacts | Apology + 1 month free / upgrade |
| 6+ contacts | Apology + 2 months free + manager outreach |

---

## Playbook 3: Post-Escalation Recovery

**Trigger:** Situation has de-escalated after being escalated to a senior agent or manager.

1. Senior agent / manager sends a personal follow-up within 24 hours
2. Acknowledge the escalation was necessary and take ownership
3. Summarize what was done — customers want proof their complaint led to action
4. Ask for feedback on the resolution
5. Offer a retention incentive based on tier
6. Flag in CRM as "Post-Escalation Watch" for 90 days

---

## Playbook 4: Delivery / Fulfillment Failure

**Trigger:** Order lost, 10+ days delayed, or arrived damaged.

1. Acknowledge within 2 hours — *"Let me look into this right now."*
2. Investigate with logistics before offering solutions
3. Give an honest answer — if it's lost, say so
4. Offer customer's choice: reship (expedited) · full refund · store credit + bonus
5. Confirm tracking number for reshipped items within 24 hours
6. Follow up on delivery day

---

## Playbook 5: Turning a Detractor into a Promoter

**Trigger:** NPS 0–5 or CSAT 1–2, but customer is still engaged.

1. Personal, non-templated outreach — use their name and reference their specific issue
2. Ask what *they* feel would make it right — then meet or exceed that
3. Deliver a "surprise and delight" gesture (credit, early feature access, founder note)
4. Follow up at Day 7, Day 30, and Day 60
5. Re-run NPS at Day 90 — target: move from Detractor → Passive (7+)
