# Response Quality Rules

> Rubrics for evaluating agent responses during QA reviews and AI response validation. Every response is scored across five dimensions.

---

## 1. Empathy (Score 1–5)

| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 1 | Jumps straight to solution — no acknowledgment |
| 2 | Boilerplate apology only ("Sorry for the inconvenience") |
| 3 | Acknowledges the issue caused a problem |
| 4 | Names the specific impact on the customer |
| 5 | Personalizes deeply — references exact situation and emotional cues |

**Target:** ≥ 4 for frustrated customers · ≥ 3 for neutral customers

**Phrases that work:** "I can completely understand why this is frustrating." / "That's not the experience you should have had."  
**Avoid:** "As per our policy..." · "Unfortunately we cannot..." · Repeated hollow "I understand your frustration"

---

## 2. Clarity (Score 1–5)

| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 1 | Vague, confusing, or contradictory |
| 3 | Covers main points but leaves some questions unanswered |
| 5 | Specific timelines, anticipates follow-up questions, clear next steps |

**Clarity checklist:** ☐ Main question answered directly ☐ Specific dates/times (not "soon") ☐ Next steps outlined ☐ No unexplained jargon

---

## 3. Tone Guidelines

| Context | Right Tone | Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| Frustrated customer | Warm, calm, direct | Formal/stiff, defensive |
| Escalated / crisis | Accountable, action-oriented | Chipper, scripted |
| Neutral inquiry | Professional, helpful | Over-casual |
| Technical issue | Clear, methodical | Condescending |

**Red flags:** Opening with "Unfortunately" · Passive voice throughout · Emoji in crisis responses · Overly long apology before any action

---

## 4. Personalization Checklist

**Must-have:**
- [ ] Customer's first name used at least once
- [ ] Specific issue referenced (not "your issue")
- [ ] Prior interactions acknowledged if relevant
- [ ] Solution tailored to their situation

**Anti-patterns:** "Dear Customer" · "We have received your query" · Recommending a solution that doesn't match their product

---

## 5. Correctness / Accuracy

- [ ] Information accurate per current knowledge base
- [ ] Quoted timelines confirmed with relevant team
- [ ] Policies referenced are up to date
- [ ] Resolution offer is within agent's authority level

---

## 6. Response Score Card

| Dimension | Score (1–5) |
|---|---|
| Empathy | /5 |
| Clarity | /5 |
| Tone | /5 |
| Personalization | /5 |
| Correctness | /5 |
| **Total** | **/25** |

**Ranges:** 21–25 🟢 Excellent · 16–20 🟡 Good · 10–15 🟠 Needs improvement · <10 🔴 Flag for coaching

---

## 7. Quick Examples

**10/10 opener (angry billing customer, 3rd contact):**
> "Hi [Name] — I owe you an apology. You've had to reach out three times and that's on us. I've reviewed your history and I can see the $47.50 overcharge. I've processed your full refund now — 2–3 business days — plus a $25 credit for your time."

**3/10 opener (same scenario):**
> "Dear Customer, we apologize for the inconvenience. Your issue has been escalated. Please allow 7–10 business days."
